The data doesn't exist on the internet

There is almost no high-quality, open data on what real humans think when they look at a product. That data lives in proprietary databases locked inside CPG companies and research firms.

LLM judges guessing what a persona would say is not ground truth. Tenor fills the gap between simulation and reality.

Why the data is credible

Nine non-negotiable rules on every study. This is what separates Tenor data from survey noise.

  • 01Between-subjects only. One condition per participant.
  • 02Distractor products. Minimum two, identical questions.
  • 03Unprompted before prompted. Associations before any hint.
  • 04Balanced options. No anchoring through asymmetry.
  • 05No leading language. Flagged and rewritten.
  • 06Stats on every comparison. Stat, p-value, effect size.
  • 07Distractor validation. No cross-condition differences.
  • 08Limitations required. Minimum four.
  • 09Open data default. OSF + DOI in the pipeline.
